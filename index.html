About Us

If you're searching for a Japanese restaurant, Hachi, situated at Km5, La Trinidad, Benguet, is the ideal destination. Offering a variety of culinary delights, Hachi restaurant provides guests with a diverse range of gastronomic experiences to indulge in.


Hachi takes pride in serving an exceptional selection of dishes, ranging from small to large plates, with a diverse array of sashimi and nigiri options flown in fresh from Japan. Our commitment to quality is reflected in the use of only the freshest ingredients to ensure that our guests savor the finest flavors.


Whether it's for dinner or a special occasion, Hachi invites you to join us and experience our unmatched hospitality. We strive to make every visit to Hachi special and memorable, ensuring that our guests receive only the best service.
